# OTBAN Wilayah VI Padang — Regional Airport Registry

This document lists the official technical specifications, communications channels, and emergency contact details for all 13 airports under the authority of **Kantor Otoritas Bandar Udara Wilayah VI Padang** (Airport Authority Region VI, Sumatra, Indonesia). 

These specifications are utilized by **PUTA-Monitor** for GIS mapping, 5 km KKOP safety buffer zone auditing, and emergency routing protocols.
