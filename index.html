About PUTA-Monitor

PUTA-Monitor was developed as a specialized utility for Kantor Otoritas Bandar Udara Wilayah VI Padang. Its primary objective is to streamline the processing, visualization, and safety compliance auditing of drone operation permits (PUTA) within the region's airspace.
